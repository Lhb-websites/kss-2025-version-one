Assessment

The Problem

Market expansion fails consistently — across industries, geographies, and operator sizes — for the same root causes. The pattern is not effort. It is sequence.

  • 01 Markets and channels are selected without demand validation — assumptions are substituted for evidence
  • 02 Distribution, partnerships, or paid channels are activated before inbound signal exists
  • 03 Capital and management attention are deployed before the opportunity is confirmed

The cost is not just financial. It is time, organizational credibility, and the market windows that close while recovery is underway. Sequencing is the discipline.
