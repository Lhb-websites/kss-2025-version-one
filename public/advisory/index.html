This Is Not Consulting.
This Is Skin in
the Game.

Advisory share arrangements are not service engagements. There is no retainer, no invoice, and no deliverable schedule. The arrangement exists because the outcome of the company is the outcome of the advisor.

KaizenStrategy brings its demand intelligence methodology, paid acquisition architecture, and senior market operator perspective directly into the company's strategic layer. The value is upstream — in sequencing, channel selection, and inbound system design — before capital is deployed in the wrong direction.

Every advisory position accepted is held to the same standard as a paying engagement: direct, evidence-based, and operated at the founder level. No account management. No generic frameworks. No hedged opinions.

01

Terms of the Arrangement

Structure

Equity-Based Compensation Only

Advisory shares issued at agreed vesting terms. No cash retainer. No hourly billing. The incentive structure is aligned with company performance — not time logged.

Commitment

Operator-Level Access

Engagement operates at the founder and operator level — direct access, direct dialogue. Not a quarterly check-in model. Not advisory in title only.

Scope

Demand, Acquisition & Inbound Systems

Core contribution spans paid acquisition strategy, demand validation, inbound system architecture, and go-to-market sequencing. Not general business mentorship.

Capacity

Two to Three Active Positions

Advisory positions are held at a deliberate limit. Depth of contribution is non-negotiable. Capacity will not be diluted across an advisory portfolio.

Geography

United States Companies Only

This offering is restricted to US-incorporated entities. Market-specific demand intelligence and channel expertise is calibrated to the US operating environment.

Duration

Standard Vesting with Cliff

Typical structure: 2-year total vest, 6-month cliff. Terms are negotiated at the engagement level based on company stage, scope of contribution, and mutual alignment.

02

Selection Criteria

Not every company is the right fit. KaizenStrategy evaluates advisory opportunities against a narrow set of criteria — prioritizing where the contribution will be material and the outcome meaningful.

01
Demand Exists — But Is Unorganized or Unconverted
The most valuable advisory position is upstream of scale — when real demand exists in the market but the inbound system has not been built to capture and convert it.
02
Paid Acquisition Is a Core Growth Channel
Companies where search, paid social, or performance channels are central to customer acquisition — and where senior strategy (not just execution) is the gap.
03
Founders Who Operate Evidence-First
The working relationship functions at the operator level. Founders who operate on assumption rather than data are not the right fit for this advisory model.
04
Pre-Seed Through Series B
The contribution is highest-value before channel commitments are locked in and before paid budgets have been deployed at scale without a validated foundation.
05
US-Incorporated and US-Market Focused
All advisory positions are limited to US entities operating in or expanding within the US market. International-first companies are not considered under this arrangement.
