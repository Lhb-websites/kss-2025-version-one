FAQ

Questions before
you book?

The 15-minute call answers everything specific to your business. These cover the most common questions we get before that call happens.

Is this only for tech companies?

No. Our clients are service businesses, e-commerce operators, agencies, consultants, healthcare practices, and local businesses. If you have a repeatable process that happens more than once a week, we can automate it.

Do I need to know how automation tools work?

Not at all. That's our job. You describe what happens in your business — the steps, the handoffs, the bottlenecks. We design and build the system. You get a walkthrough when it's done.

How fast can you get something running?

Most single workflows are live within 3–5 business days of the scoping call. Larger builds (Full Operations, Scale retainer) have a 10-business-day priority window.

What if I only have 15 minutes right now?

That's exactly the call. 15 minutes is enough for us to tell you what we'd automate first, what it would save you per week, and which tier makes sense for your situation. No deck. No pitch.

What tools do you build on?

Zapier, Make, n8n, Power Automate, and GoHighLevel — depending on what fits your stack. We also integrate OpenAI and other AI models for content, scoring, and data enrichment workflows.

Can I start project-based and move to a retainer later?

Yes — and it's a natural progression. Most clients start with a Starter or Bundle project to see the work firsthand, then move to a retainer once they know they want ongoing builds and maintenance.
